About Algambino motors

We are an all in one motor services company with certification from the motor vehicles quality assurance state department and comply to all the regulations pertaining to the cap-142 of the Kenyan constitution.Our operations include: dealing of motor vehicles; both new and used, Mechanical service and maintainance and have started out a car wash franchise dubbed 'Sparkle' located through several locations countrywide. Visit our office located on 12th Street along Moi Ave, Mombasa-Kenya

We boast in having the best quality of service not just the best motors in town, visit one of our locations today and be sure of having a story to tell. Our vehicles are thoroughly inspected and checked by our engineers before we sell them to you be it new or used and even our used cars are properly refurbished and cleaned up to standard using our newly equiped car vaccum dispensation equipment and you wont have to worry about your allergies to dust or animal fur from the previous user.

To get your car engine serviced or get your brake lights fixed, dont look further as we also have a motor garage located at our warehouse-02situated along Ferry Links rd and dont leave your dash dusted, just visit our Sparkle car wash along Mwingu-wa-Mbindu 54th St and shine your way out!

To make a purchase please visit our office location in town or login to our customers portal for your online purchase and we shall contact you via email and you shall recieve your invoice details. New customers can sign up to create an account and enjoy our exclusive online discounts.
